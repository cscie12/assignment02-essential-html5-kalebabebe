Affordable Housing Overlay

Adopted in October 2020, the 100%-Affordable Housing Overlay (AHO) aims to accelerate the development of affordable homes cost-effectively. It permits denser affordable housing in areas with limited affordable options. By streamlining approval processes, AHO enables quicker creation of new affordable units, improving the efficiency of public funding use.

BEUDO

BEUDO (Building Energy Use Disclosure Ordinance) is a crucial initiative since 2014 to combat greenhouse gas emissions primarily from commercial and large residential buildings, which account for 80% of the city's emissions. BUEDO mandates annual energy consumption reporting and requires emissions reduction measures, aiming for Net Zero Emissions in large commercial buildings by 2035. Aligned with Cambridge's sustainability goals, the city oversees reporting, due annually by May 1st. For updates, residents can join the BUEDO email list or contact BUEDO@cambridgema.gov.
